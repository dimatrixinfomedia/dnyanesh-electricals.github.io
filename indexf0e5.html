We are a family owned electrical contracting company servicing the East Valley and Phoenix area. We have been working since 2006 to provide our customers with the best experience possible.

Our electricians bring years of experience to the job. We specialize in lighting fixture upgrades and installations, service calls, remodels and new construction.
